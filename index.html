About Me

I am an Air Force Air National Guard Veteran with deployments to the AOR, PACAF, and a TDY to USAFE. In the AOR, I focused on systems administration, process streamlining, and vulnerability management—building automation via PowerShell scripts and handling manual patching due to MCM/software center outages.

I was a key member of a Comm Fly-Away Kit (CFK) team that successfully deployed SATCOM capabilities to a remote HQ site within 12 hours.

In PACAF, I led ACAS scanning operations and deployed their SolarWinds monitoring infrastructure. While in USAFE, I built three Splunk servers and created dashboards for failed logins, device counts (computers, switches, servers), and a geo map showing device locations using coordinate lookups.

Currently, I serve as a Cybersecurity Analyst at the Pentagon, supporting Security Assessment and Validation (SAV), CCRI/CORA inspections, and aiding organizations in completing their ATO packages.
